Description:

Chicken fry is saucy indian flavoured chicken dipped in sauces and spices

This classic and comforting meal cooks under 45 minutes and serves with chapati and rice

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 kg chicken
  • 1/2 kg onion
  • 250 gm tomato

Steps:

  1. give water
  2. boil it upto 100
  3. Add spices
